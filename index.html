01 Featured Project

CREDA — AI Skill Verification Live

Resumes don't prove anything. So we built this.

The Problem

People list skills on their resume that they've barely used. There's no good way to check if someone actually built something or just followed a tutorial.

What I Built

CREDA pulls your GitHub repos, reads your code, and generates an interview about it. The questions are specific enough that you can't fake your way through.

What Happened

200+ students tried it. Most of them came back to update their profiles. Turns out people actually want a way to prove what they know.

What I Learned

Writing prompts for one person is easy. Writing prompts that work for 200 different codebases is a completely different problem. That's what this project taught me.
